About Me

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Science at IIIT Hyderabad. Prior to this, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering. My goal is to become a software engineer who brings innovation to the technologies shaping the future, particularly Artificial Intelligence.

During my academic journey, I worked on various exciting projects, including an image caption generator using VGG-16 and a mini shell in my undergraduate years. These experiences have fueled my passion for problem-solving and building technologies that make a real-world impact.

Projects

Image Caption Generator Using VGG-16

This project involved developing an image captioning model that uses the VGG-16 architecture to analyze images and generate relevant captions. It integrates deep learning techniques to bridge the gap between visual data and language, a key challenge in AI.

Mini Shell

I developed a mini shell recently pursuing my master's. The project involved implementing fundamental shell functionalities, such as command execution, pipelines, and I/O redirection. It was an enriching experience that deepened my understanding of operating systems and low-level programming.
